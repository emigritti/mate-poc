# Why This Framework Is Accenture‑Compliant (One‑Pager)

## Executive Summary
This repository defines a **secure, responsible, AI‑assisted development framework** aligned with Accenture standards.
It is designed for internal development, enablement, and reference architectures.

---

## Alignment Highlights

### Secure Use of Gen AI
- Explicit prohibition of client and confidential data
- Clear data usage boundary

### Responsible AI
- Transparency, explainability, and human‑in‑the‑loop

### Application & Agentic Security
- Secure‑by‑default development
- AI‑specific controls (prompt injection, tool restriction)

### OWASP Alignment
- OWASP ASVS & Top 10 checklists
- Mapping between unit tests, security tests, and risks

---

## Governance & Audit Readiness
- ADR‑based decision traceability
- Mandatory test and security gates
- Explicit compliance documentation

**Result:** AI accelerates delivery without weakening trust, security, or governance.
